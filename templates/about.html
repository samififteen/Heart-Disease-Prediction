All About Heart Diseases

Your heart health matters. Learn the risks, causes, and how you can protect yourself!

What Are Heart Diseases?

Heart diseases refer to a wide range of conditions that affect the heart, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, heart defects, and more. It is one of the leading causes of death worldwide, but early diagnosis and preventive measures can save lives.

Causes of Heart Diseases

Heart diseases can be caused by various factors, including:

  • Unhealthy lifestyle choices like smoking or poor diet.
  • High cholesterol or high blood pressure.
  • Stress, obesity, and lack of physical activity.
  • Genetic predisposition or family history of heart conditions.

Common Symptoms

Here are some common symptoms of heart diseases that you should not ignore:

  • Chest pain or discomfort.
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
  • Fatigue, dizziness, or fainting spells.
  • Swelling in the legs, ankles, or feet.

Precautions and Preventive Measures

Prevention is better than cure. Follow these steps to keep your heart healthy:

  • Eat a heart-healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly to keep your heart strong.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Manage your stress through activities like meditation or yoga.
  • Regularly monitor your blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and blood sugar.
